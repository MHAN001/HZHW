Spring Festival Gala

Introduction

Origin

The CCTV Spring Festival Gala is a comprehensive cultural evening hosted by CCTV to celebrate the New Year on New Year's Eve. Originated in 1979 and officially opened in 1983.

Art Forms

The CCTV Spring Festival Gala covers various art forms such as sketches, songs, songs and dances, acrobatics, magic, drama, comic drama, etc.

Evaluation

The Spring Festival Gala is performed in a manner that is both ornamental and artistic. It interprets the human world, records the bittersweet taste of life, and spreads the attention and expectations of the people.

Background

    From the first Spring Festival Gala, the Spring Festival Gala has already passed more than 30 years. Generally speaking, during these 30 years, the Spring Festival Gala has gone through roughly three stages: from the thriving and dominant figure in the 1980s to the exploration and growth in the 1990s, and to the maturity of the new century. test. A fixed viewing time, a relatively stable program form, helps viewers generate viewing inertia. After nearly 30 years of accumulation, the Spring Festival Gala formed a relatively stable "Spring Festival Gala" model and created its own unique cultural brand.

    As one of the oldest traditional festivals in China, the Spring Festival condenses the Chinese people's ethical feelings and feelings of acceptance, demonstrating the profoundness of the Chinese civilization and the profound Chinese culture. Joy, peace, and happiness are the common aspirations of all Chinese. The Spring Festival Gala reflects these wishes through the culture that the program carries and meets the needs of the audience. CCTV chose New Year's Eve this special day to hold a national holiday celebration party. Naturally, "unity," "joy," and "harmony" are its unchanging themes.
