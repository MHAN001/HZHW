Spring Festival Foods

Introduction

Dumplings

The homonym of the dumplings is the moment when the New Year meets the old year. Eating dumplings for the Chinese New Year means a good day. In addition, dumplings are shaped like ingots. Making dumplings means to contain Fortune.

Glue Pudding

The Glue Pudding symbolize a better family reunion. Eating Glue Pudding means that the new year is a happy family and a round. In some areas in the south, people will be used to eating Glue Pudding instead of dumplings during the Spring Festival.

Rice Cake

The rice cake has red, yellow, and white colors, which symbolize money and treasure. The homonym of the the rice cake in Chinese means "higher and better year after year." It means that people's work and life improve year after year.

Spring Roll

In southern China, Spring Festival does not eat dumplings but spring rolls. Eating spring rolls during the spring festival have the meaning of celebrating the spring and praying for a good harvest.

Longevity Noodle

Longevity Noodle is also called birthday noodles, give the old men a Longevity Noodle on the birthday means wish them health and longevity. Eat a long face during the Spring Festival, foreshadowing all year round!

Fish

The homonym of eating fish in the New Year “has fish every year”, which means that every household has fish every year. Another homonym represents a prosperous life and there is excess wealth and food every year!
